** Story  **
Once upon a time in a forest, there was a monkey who lived on a jamun tree. And in the same forest there lived a 
crocodile and his wife.One day the crocodile came to the bank of the river and rested under the tree. The kind 
hearted monkey gave him some fruits.The crocodile came again next day for more fruits, as he loved them and
they became great friends. Then the crocodile tells about the monkey to his wife.
